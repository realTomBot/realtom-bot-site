Privacy Policy

Effective: March 17, 2026

.md Viewer is a free Markdown reader app published by TomBot. This policy explains how we handle your data — the short version: we don't collect any.

Data Collection

.md Viewer does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No crash reporting services
  • No advertising frameworks
  • No network requests of any kind
  • No account creation or sign-in

File Access

The app reads Markdown files (.md, .markdown) that you explicitly open via the iOS document picker or the "Open with" system action. Files are read locally on your device. When you toggle a checkbox, the change is written back to the same local file. No file contents are ever transmitted off your device.

Third-Party Services

None. The app operates entirely offline with zero external dependencies.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page with a new effective date.
