Tombot Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tombot (“Tombot,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information for our websites, mobile apps, and related services, including apps distributed through the Apple App Store.

Information we collect

The exact information depends on which Tombot product you use. We may collect:

  • Account and contact information, such as your email address or profile details if you create an account, join a waitlist, request support, or otherwise contact us.
  • App activity and preferences, such as feature usage, settings, saved preferences, and content you choose to submit in an app.
  • Device, diagnostics, and analytics data, such as device type, operating system, app version, crash logs, performance data, pages viewed, referral source, and approximate region.
  • Wallet or blockchain-related information, if a product lets you connect a wallet or interact with on-chain services. Public blockchain activity may be visible to others by design.
  • Support communications, including messages you send us and related metadata needed to respond.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information unless you choose to provide it.

How we use information

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve Tombot products.
  • Process your requests, support messages, app interactions, and preferences.
  • Monitor reliability, security, fraud prevention, abuse prevention, and debugging.
  • Understand product usage and improve user experience.
  • Comply with legal, safety, and platform obligations.

Analytics and third-party services

Our website uses Google Analytics. Our apps and services may also rely on service providers for hosting, analytics, diagnostics, authentication, payments, cloud infrastructure, AI functionality, customer support, and App Store distribution. These providers process information on our behalf or according to their own terms when you use their services directly.

Links from Tombot to third-party services, including any.markets, app stores, wallet providers, block explorers, or social platforms, are governed by those services’ own privacy policies.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers, legal or safety authorities when required, transaction counterparties where necessary for a feature you request, or in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets.

Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described above, including product operation, security, legal compliance, accounting, dispute resolution, and legitimate business records.

Your choices

  • You can stop using an app or uninstall it at any time.
  • You can adjust device permissions in your operating system settings.
  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of personal information by contacting us.
  • If a Tombot app supports accounts, you can request account deletion by emailing us from the address associated with the account.
For privacy, data access, or deletion requests, contact: tombot@agentmail.to

Children

Tombot products are not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided us personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

International users

We may process information in the United States and other countries where we or our service providers operate. By using Tombot products, you understand that information may be transferred to locations with different data protection laws than your country.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our products evolve. When we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.

Contact

Questions or privacy requests: tombot@agentmail.to
